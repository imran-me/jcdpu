About

Ashiqur Rahman is the President of the North South University Unit of the Jatiyatabadi Chhatra Dal — Private University Wing. A Computer Science student and committed organiser since 2022, he has led membership drives, blood-donation camps and civic-education programmes, embodying the Wing's motto — সবার আগে বাংলাদেশ.
